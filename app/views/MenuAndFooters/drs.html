Apply to update or create a registered title

You can use this service to submit an AP1 form to update an existing title.

This service can be used for applications of up to 25 titles where the ‘same proprietors are registered against each tile.

Coronavirus (COVID-19): temporary rules on identity

HIM Land Registry has introduced a range of temporary measures for verifying identity during the coronavirus (COVID-19) outbreak.

‘The measures are set out in Practice Guide 67 (opens in new tab), which accompanies Practice Guide 67 (opens in new tab).

Before you start

You will need to provide:

  • title numbers
  • applicant details
  • scanned documents, if needed

You cannot use this service to:

  • register a transfer of charge
  • register a transfer by operation of law on death
  • register a transfer of residue
  • register a transfer of part applications
  • submit applications for personal representation
  • submit applications against a pending new title which involves a transfer of charge
  • merge multiple title numbers

For these applications, please use the Document Registration Service.

For applications of more than 25 titles

You cannot currently use the Digital Registration Service for applications of more than 28 titles. For these applications:

  • use the Document Registration Service for applications of 26-50 titles
  • contact the Bulk Application Team for applications of over 50 titles
