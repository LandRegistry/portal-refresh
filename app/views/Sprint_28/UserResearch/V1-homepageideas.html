Homepage ideas

  • Content will be reorganised depending on the action being performed e.g. Create, Share, Request
  • Simplified look and feel, that can be universally adopted across the portal platform
  • A dynamic layout - allowing the most relevant information to be presented to each user. Layouts will be:
    • Affected by user roles
    • Or customised through the use of widgets
  • Video guides designed to teach new and existing users how to use different services
  • A section that informs the user who their BUA is, along with an easy way to contact them
  • The ability to download the latest available PDFs
  • Previews of outstanding requisitions and the option to reply to them
  • Services that link out/away from the portal will be clearly signposted in a new section
  • A live twitter feed to inform users of service status, disruptions or maintenance
